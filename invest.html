Investment

  • ● We select a tiny fraction of the farmers we review, ensuring each offering is of the highest quality. Each farm and its legal title are placed into a unique production contract between BAMBOU and Farmer/ Entrepreneur.

    ● We charge a flat annual administration fee of 0.75% (of overall farm value) that is typically deducted from the income of the farm.

  • ● After 6 months, each plantation is independently valued, grouped to create the SPV, and divided into units of the total partnership interests.

    ● BAMBOU invites Investors to invest in the partnership through the SPV, semi-annually at the beginning of every planting season (usually in March & September).

Market Considerations

Bamboo is a strong and fast-growing material, having been used for thousands of years because of its versatility and sustainability. The global bamboo market size was valued at $68.8 billion in 2018 and is expected to grow at a compounded annual growth rate of 5% from 2019 to 2025.

Experienced Sponsor & Proven Partners

The BPE team has experience growing bamboo, as they have parcels of land already producing bamboo. The company has infrastructure in place from their existing bamboo operation and the site selected is in a favorable climate and soil for optimal plant growth.

Positive Environmental Impact

Bamboo is a grass that grows almost a meter a day. After the first three years, it is harvested annually without the need of replanting. Bamboo is a sustainable resource that can grow under a range of climatic conditions, and improves soil nutrients while preventing erosion. Bamboo can add 6-8 inches of humus to soil per year.

Demand for cleaner sources of energy using bamboo as biomass are increasing the demand and driving the market price higher. Bamboo gas can be used as a substitute for petroleum. Bamboo charcoal is an excellent fuel for cooking. Charcoal is used as a deodorant, purifier, disinfectant, medicine, agricultural chemical and absorbent of pollution and excessive moisture.
