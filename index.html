BAMBO PLANTING EMPOWERMENT LTD

Bambou Planting Empowerment Ltd is, farmland operator and investment fund which develops bamboo-based agroforestry projects in Uganda. We identify underutilized areas of land to develop sound and sustainable bamboo-based agrisilvipasture projects combining economic benefits with high environmental & social returns. We currently operate in Wakiso and Apac District
