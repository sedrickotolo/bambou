About Us

Our Mission

To become the leading bamboo plantation company in Uganda
by adopting Agrisilvipasture production systems and an aggressive Collaboration strategy with
Smallholder farmers/ ranchers.

What We Do
01

Plant

We aid farmers with quality materials, training, and support for land restoration, carbon capture, and sustainable growth and development

02

Grow

We promote crop-livestock integration and safe farm caretaking with job creation in underserved areas, and career growth.

03

Process

During growth, we promote crop-livestock integration and farm care. We harvest bamboo, enhancing sustainability and productivity.

04

Invest

We transfer production contracts to SPVs for upfront payments, recycling initial investments. LPs fund maintenance and scaling

How We Do It

We identify underutilized areas and acquire right-of-use to adopt Agrisilvipasture production systems. We enroll and contract smallholder farmers/ ranchers and provide training, planting inputs, startup livestock, maintenance support, harvesting services, and a guaranteed market. We harvest, process and market the produce and value-added products; and facilitate effective circulation of capital to our partner “bamboopreneurs”.


Bambou Planting Empowerment Ltd is on track to becoming the leading bamboo plantation company in Uganda by adopting Agrisilvipasture production systems and an aggressive Collaboration strategy with Smallholder farmers
