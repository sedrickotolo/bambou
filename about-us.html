About Us

Bamboo Ascent

We are on track to becoming the leading bamboo plantation company in Uganda. This has been achieved by adopting an aggressive collaboration strategy with Smallholder farmers.

Bamboo Synergy

We promote bamboo-based agroforestry systems by empowering farmers through propagation and supply of quality planting materials; awareness and training and support on plantation design and management best practices; harvesting, processing and marketing of bamboo produce and value-added products; and facilitating effective circulation of capital to the “bamboopreneurs”.
